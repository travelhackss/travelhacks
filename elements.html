What is a travel guide by Travel Hacks ?

A travel guide or tourist guide is a collection of valuable information to prepare your trip to a destination. It is usually known as a book popularized by the famous guidebooks or Michelin guides, but it is now found in online guide format via our app.

Presentation of our travel guides.

These travel guides are intended to give you the best information on the major travel destinations around the world. You will find advice on the budget, advice on places to stay (hotels, inns), things to see and do (activities, outings) and gastronomic specialties. These guides are regularly enriched and updated. The role of the guide is also to give you ideas for outings and things to do that are off the beaten path (trips and unusual places) or that it is unthinkable to miss if you go there. Thus you do not waste your time on the spot to inform you for days on what is absolutely necessary or in terms of activities.
